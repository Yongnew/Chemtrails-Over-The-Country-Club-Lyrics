About Chemtrails Over The Country Club

Chemtrails over the Country Club is the seventh studio album by American singer-songwriter Lana Del Rey.
It was released on March 19, 2021, by Interscope and Polydor Records.

Chemtrails over the Country Club is described as a folk, country folk, and Americana record.
According to Del Rey, the album is about her friends and family.
Other themes featured on the album are themes of escapism, love, heartbreak, and nostalgia, while encompassing her usual references to Americana, alongside emotions of disillusionment.
